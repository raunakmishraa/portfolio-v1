About

Hi, this is Raunak Mishra, a student of Higher Secondary at St. Xavier's College, Maitighar, Kathmandu, Nepal. I'm very fond of Physics and Computers. I'm a 17-year-old guy. The thing of which, I'm very fond of are: Physics, Computers, Gadgets and recent technological advancements. Also, my favorite games are cricket and football for outdoor and ludo, chess and carrom for indoor.
Some of my works like "COVID-19 Spread Breaking Device for Public Places" make me feel proud of myself. Altough I haven't experienced life much, I try my best to know more and more about life. I always enjoy my own company. On this website I just share some of my experiences, works, and projects. Furthermore, make everyone able to directly connect with me.

Currently, I'm studying in Class 12 in St. Xavier's College affiliated to NEB, Nepal. And also I'm the vice-president of SXC Physics Club in my college.
